# Trawlist — Chrome Web Store Listing

## Summary / Short Description
Rate any YouTube video you watch, without leaving the page.

---

## Detailed Description

Trawlist adds a sleek, non-intrusive rating and review control directly onto YouTube video pages. Rate videos on a 5-star scale, save your favorite videos, and write reviews seamlessly as you watch.

### Features:
- 🌟 **5-Star Community Ratings**: Rate videos directly next to YouTube's native action bar.
- ❤️ **Like & Bookmark**: Keep track of videos you love.
- 💬 **Write & Read Reviews**: Share your thoughts with the community.
- ⚡ **Lightweight & Fast**: Built for speed with minimal impact on browser performance.

---

### Privacy Commitment:
Trawlist strictly reads active YouTube video URLs/IDs solely to fetch and attach your ratings, likes, and reviews. We do **NOT** track, collect, or store your personal browsing history outside of YouTube, and we never sell your data to third parties.
