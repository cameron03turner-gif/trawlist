# Privacy Policy for Trawlist

**Last Updated**: July 2026

Trawlist ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our Chrome extension operates and how your data is handled.

---

## 1. Information We Collect and How It Is Used

- **Video Identification**: The extension reads the active YouTube video URL and Video ID strictly for the purpose of attaching, saving, and displaying ratings, likes, and reviews for that specific video.
- **Account Data**: If you sign in, we securely store your email address and authentication session tokens via Supabase to sync your ratings across devices.

---

## 2. What We DO NOT Collect or Track

- **No General Browsing History**: Trawlist does **NOT** collect, track, store, or transmit your personal browsing history outside of YouTube.
- **No Third-Party Tracking**: We do not use third-party tracking scripts, analytics trackers, or data brokers.
- **No Selling of Data**: We do not sell, rent, or monetize your personal data or activity.

---

## 3. Data Storage and Security

All user ratings, likes, and reviews are transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections directly to our secure Supabase backend. Session credentials are stored locally in your browser using Chrome's secure storage API (`chrome.storage.local`).
